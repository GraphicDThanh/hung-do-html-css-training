Our story

Who are we?

Hi! We are Tom & Kay — two passionate engineers of user experience. Building things people want makes us happy!

Kay is a Ex-Googler and Tom is a startup veteran. We started VanGoux because it didn’t make sense to us that when a designer creates a design, engineers have to code it from scratch.

What’s up with the name?

Pronounced as Van Gogh — its a word play on Van Gogh meets UX.

Vincent transformed the pain of his tormented life into ecstatic beauty. Our name is a our way of honoring his profound work.
